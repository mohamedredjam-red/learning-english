📖 Lesson 1: Apologising

📖 Lesson Content ▼

Transcript:

Ana: Hi! I'm Ana. Welcome to What to Say!

Do you know what to say when you want to apologise? Listen out for useful language for apologising.

Paul: Noelia! I am so sorry! Are you OK?
Noelia: I think so. That was lucky.
Paul: I'm really sorry! I didn't see you!
Noelia: That's all right. I shouldn't have been running.
Paul: Still, it was my fault and I'm sorry.
Noelia: I'm sorry too.
Paul: Honestly, it's fine. Don't worry about it.

📌 Useful phrases:

  • I'm so sorry.
  • I'm really sorry.
  • That's all right.
  • It was my fault.
  • I'm sorry too.
  • Don't worry about it.
